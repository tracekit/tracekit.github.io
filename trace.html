TraceKit Tracing Policy

All location data saved by your device(s) or app(s) stays on your device, and what TCX/GPX file you upload into the TraceKit is used to compare your previous locations you have visited and locations visited by a CoronaVirus infected person. After the comparison is made, the TCX/GPX file is deleted. Basically that's how it works, nothing more or less, because that's the way it should be.

Furthermore TraceKit doesn't use any type of third party analytics, or trackers, so there's no risk of your data leaking to third parties. This means there's no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no Mixpanel, Fabric, Mailchimp, etc — none whatsoever.

Conduct TraceKit

- An email address is needed to receive the TraceKit results. All information submitted is deleted once an TraceKit has been completed

- If you don't know how to export a TCX or GCX file from your smartwatch, fitness device or fitness app, please visit our FAQ
