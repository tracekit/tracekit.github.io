TraceKit Privacy Policy

Everything TraceKit does stays on your device, and what you see is literally what you get, and nothing more or less, because that's the way it should be.

Longer Version: Everything TraceKit does stays on your device and apps, data is transmitted to any of our servers only when you upload a TCX or GPX file. Once the trace has been completed TraceKit deletes all files. TraceKit doesn't use any type of third party analytics, or trackers, so there's no risk of your data leaking to third parties. This means there's no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no Mixpanel, Fabric, Mailchimp, etc — none what so ever.

Information We Collect

TraceKit collects nothing. It all stays on your device. TraceKit on the other hand collects report information to verify and to know known locations an infected person may have visited.
